Resumes 📄

When making a resume, it is essential to include many things to ensure that your resume is professional, and reppresents who you are as a person.

  1. Contact Information is important on a resume, with things such as an email and a phone number
  2. A professional 2-3 sentence summary of who you are and our skills and experience
  3. Listing your previous education (high school, post secondary)
  4. Include all previous job experience and also voulenteer experience, using verbs like "managed, developed" to make it sound professional
  5. Showcase relevant skills that you have, both hard and soft skills
  6. Highlight any certificates or awards that you recieved, both educational and extracurricular
  7. Customize your resume to ensure it is specific to each job you apply for
  8. Ensure that your resume is in a PDF format to ensure universal compatability across all software

Using these 8 resume tips will ensure that your resume will stand out among other resumes, and ensure that you will land the position that you are looking for, and if you ever have any other questions, don't hesitate to ask others online.

Skills 🎓

As a student, an amazing extra curricular activity that you can do is to learn new skills. Even if you don't have a use for the skills yet, skills that you learn can be useful in your future. Some examples of skills that you can learn are:

3D Design - Blender Game Development - Unity Programming - Python Graphic Design - Inkscape Web Dev - HTML5

All of these skills can be learned for free, and most of the software mentioned is open source, meaning that you will always be able to use them for free. Although Unity is not open source, it is a fantastic free game engine, and easy to learn. Follow the links for installation guides.
